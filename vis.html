Visualizing data is a challenging task due to multiple reasons: One reason is selecting the most fitting visualizationtechnique. Data can be presented in different ways and choosing the right visualization technique is vital for understanding whichinformation the data is representing. As technology is on the rise these challenges become more important to be tackled. Using thewrong visualization techniques can result to people misunderstanding the data and as as result come to a wrong conclusion. Thispaper describes two visualization techniques, the node-link diagram and the adjacency matrix, and how these visualizations can bemade dynamic. Dynamic visualizations are relevant since data is often collected over a period of time. Dynamic visualizations help todiscover trends and changes in the data. Different methods can be used to make a visualization dynamic, such as animation or the small multiples method. Furthermore, this report describes different ways of interacting with the visualization to make it fit to theusers needs. The methods include making use of a time slider, dragging nodes around, selecting certain data points to focus on orselecting clusters of data to be presented in the visualization
